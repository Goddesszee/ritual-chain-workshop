Commitment Formula
How your answer is protected
commitment =
  keccak256(
    answer +
    salt +
    msg.sender +
    bountyId
  )
salt — random bytes32, prevents brute-force
msg.sender — prevents commitment replay attacks
bountyId — prevents cross-bounty reuse
