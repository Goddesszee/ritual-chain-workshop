How it works
1
Create and Fund Set a question, rubric, and two deadlines. Prize is locked in the contract.
2
Participants Commit Submit a keccak256 hash of their answer. The actual answer stays hidden.
3
Reveal Phase After the commit deadline, participants reveal. The contract verifies the hash.
4
Ritual AI Judges A single batch LLM call scores all revealed answers against the rubric.
5
Winner Paid Owner finalizes the winner. Prize sent instantly on-chain.
Submit Commitment
Your answer is hashed locally — nothing is stored on-chain yet
Your answer stays completely hidden until the reveal phase. Only a cryptographic hash is submitted.
Save your salt — required to reveal
Commitment Formula
commitment =
  keccak256(
    answer ||
    salt ||
    msg.sender ||
    bountyId
  )
salt — random bytes32 prevents brute-force
msg.sender — prevents replay attacks
bountyId — prevents cross-bounty reuse
Reveal Answer
Only available after the commit deadline passes
Reveal Rules
Reveal opens only after the commit deadline closes
Answer must match your commitment hash exactly
You can only reveal once per bounty
Unrevealed answers are ineligible for judging
Reveal window closes at the reveal deadline
